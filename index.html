Bio

Miguel Ballesteros is a computer scientist and computational linguist specializing in the intersection of natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning. His research spans various areas of NLP and language modeling.

Work History and Education

Miguel is currently an AI Architect (Applied Scientist) at Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI). Previously, he worked as a Principal Applied Scientist at Amazon Web Services (AWS), and has also held positions at IBM, Carnegie Mellon University (CMU), and Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF). He has taught undergraduate and graduate courses at both CMU and UPF. Miguel earned his Ph.D. in 2012 from Universidad Complutense de Madrid, completing two research stays at Uppsala University.

Service and Research Publications

Miguel has served as an Area Chair for multiple conferences and is an Action Editor for Transactions of the Association for Computational Linguistics, Computational Linguistics, and the ACL Rolling Review. He has authored over 70 peer-reviewed papers and articles, which have been cited more than 12,500 times according to Google Scholar.
